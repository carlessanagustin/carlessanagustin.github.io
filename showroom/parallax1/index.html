Welcome

Born in Barcelona in 1977 with a head full of dreams, artist inside but engineer by training. Carles believes in the beauty of passion.

A lover of technology applied to the visual arts. Carles knows how to enjoy work and life, combined with creativity and energy. A practical and innovative professional, who gives many turns to a project until it is perfect from the technical point of view, but able to find that aesthetic detail that will make the difference.

Lover of photography, tireless traveler, dreamer and visionary. Carles invites you to discover and share his own world from these pages.
